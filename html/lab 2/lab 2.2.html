Scrambled Eggs

Egg are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has meltef add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20s
